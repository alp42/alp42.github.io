About alp42

alp42 is a pattern colleciton for agile and lean software development.

alp42 is a collection of practices and patterns for agile and lean software development:

alp42:

  1. helps you to find the right agile aproach for your project

  2. is not bound to any framework or methodology

  3. supports both business and technical stakeholders

  4. organizes patterns and practices for various

  5. Free and open-source, contributions welcome

  6. Grounded in practice , proven approaches, backed by serious research

Authored by the agilepattern community, lead by Gerrit Beine <mail@gerritbeine.de>. alp42 is based on the concept and tools of aim42.
